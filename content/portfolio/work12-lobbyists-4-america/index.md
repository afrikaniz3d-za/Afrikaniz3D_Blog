I chose the Lobbyists 4 America project. The project was done on the [**Community Edition**](https://community.cloud.databricks.com/) of the **Databricks** platform. I used both SQL and Python to carry out the exercise.

I covered a lot of new areas in this project, including having to use machine learning to work with subjective data. It also gave me some insight into how social media is used to shape thought and perception as well as how some of the strategies employed aren't as direct/obvious as one would assume. On top of that, it also helps to explain some of the decisions made by social networks (like Twitter) sometimes change how algorithms work and why some of those changes aren't shared wit the public/creators.
