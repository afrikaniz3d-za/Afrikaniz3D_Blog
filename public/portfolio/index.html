BRAUBERRY [Brand Mash-Up Project]

The Brand Mash-Up series was one of the first bigger projects on my YouTube channel that I started as a means of both expanding on what I had been doing as well as learning new tools such as Blender. The idea for BRAUBERRY was a response to the need for being careful when handling intellectual properties I didn’t own in the case they might endanger my YouTube channel. It was also a fun opportunity to see what could come about from merging two seemingly non-related brands.

Data Explorations using R Studio

The purpose of this data exploration is to apply and the practice the skills gained from the Google Data Analytics Professional Certification. The data being explored relates to South African energy, primarily electricity. This exercise will be replicated on other tools later on such as Power BI and SQL. The datasets are mostly in .csv format and were collected from the following sources: Eskom World Bank Energy Data Regulatory Indicator for Sustainable Energy (RISE)
