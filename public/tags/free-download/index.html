Been Busy

I got to learn my modest way around geometry nodes while developing HEXES Mini Pack 04: Electronic Devices. I started work on it in January and was having trouble deciding how many to produce and the style to make them in. Using geonodes made it feel more flexible and user-friendly. The first project was Sensor 21, based on an air quality dataset I explored for a portfolio project. Made a walkthrough video to show my process for the antenna.

Still

Might just be the combination of things I’ve been focusing on lately, but finding a good fit is shifting more and more. Found a way to make the data explorations more interesting while also getting to make new stuff as well as following through with older ideas. Nothing goes to waste.

Nutvember 2022

A year ago I created the Nutvember kitbashing set of nuts and bolts to… celebrate(?) No Nut November (NNN). Also known as Movember it was something I’d been casually familiar with and I wanted to change that. Read some papers, some very old and others recent. Masturbation is what I read the most on because I wanted to find conversations on the act that weren’t focused on the psycho-sexual parts explored by the Freuds or the Kinseys.
